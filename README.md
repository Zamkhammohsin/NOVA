# NOVA
Nova is a modern, minimalist desktop clock widget that makes your desktop look futuristic and sleek. With fully customizable colors, layout, and size, Nova adapts perfectly to your style and workflow.

Features:

✨ Dark theme with subtle purple-blue gradient – smooth, futuristic look

🎨 Customizable layout & size – place it anywhere on your desktop

🖥️ Minimal and lightweight – designed for performance and elegance

🚀 Instantly usable – comes as a ready-to-run .exe

Why Nova?
Nova is more than a clock—it’s a statement on your desktop. Clean lines, modern UI, and futuristic aesthetics make it a joy to use and show off.
